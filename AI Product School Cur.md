# AI Product School Curriculum  
### Engineering, AI Systems, and Technical Writing

This document defines a **comprehensive curriculum framework** for an **AI Product School and Technology Company** responsible for training engineers capable of **designing, building, deploying, and documenting real technology products**.

The curriculum focuses on developing professionals who can:

- build AI systems
- design scalable software
- create technical products
- write clear technical documentation
- launch real-world technology platforms

The structure follows a **first-principles engineering model**, beginning with computational foundations and progressing toward AI product development and technical communication.

# 1. Curriculum Philosophy

Most technology education systems teach isolated technical skills.

This curriculum is built around **systems-level capability development**.

Students must learn how to:

- understand complex systems
- design technology architecture
- implement working software
- deploy real products
- communicate technical systems clearly

The curriculum emphasizes **engineering depth, product thinking, and communication clarity**.

---

# 2. Learning Architecture

The training model is divided into **five core engineering pillars**.

```

Computational Foundations
↓
Software Engineering Systems
↓
Artificial Intelligence Engineering
↓
AI Product Engineering
↓
Technical Writing & Documentation

```

These pillars ensure that graduates can **build technology systems and communicate them effectively**.
