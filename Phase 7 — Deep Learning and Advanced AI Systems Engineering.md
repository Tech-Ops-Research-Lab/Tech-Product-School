# Phase 7 — Deep Learning and Advanced AI Systems Engineering
## AI Engineering Product School Curriculum

Phase 7 focuses on **advanced deep learning architectures and AI systems engineering**.  
Engineers consolidate prior machine learning and AI system knowledge to **build sophisticated AI products and systems at scale**.

The primary objective is to produce engineers capable of:

- designing and deploying **cutting-edge deep learning models**  
- building **complex AI systems**  
- integrating AI into **production-grade technology products**  
- solving high-impact problems with **state-of-the-art AI solutions**  

Learning progression:

```

Advanced Neural Networks
↓
Sequence & Transformer Models
↓
Generative AI Systems
↓
Multi-Modal AI Integration
↓
AI System Scaling & Deployment
↓
Optimization & Reliability Engineering

```

# 1. Phase Objectives

Engineers completing Phase 7 are capable of:

- building **state-of-the-art deep learning architectures**  
- integrating multiple AI modalities into production systems  
- deploying high-performance AI models at scale  
- designing AI systems for **high reliability, low latency, and high throughput**  

---

# 2. Advanced Neural Networks

Topics include:

- Deep Feedforward Networks  
- Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) at scale  
- Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) and LSTMs for sequential data  
- Residual Networks (ResNets) and DenseNets  
- Attention mechanisms and self-attention layers  

Applications:

- advanced image recognition  
- video analytics  
- sequence modeling  

---

# 3. Sequence Models and Transformers

Key topics:

- Transformer architecture fundamentals  
- Encoder-Decoder models  
- Attention and Multi-Head Attention mechanisms  
- Applications: NLP, time-series forecasting, recommendation systems  

Engineers learn to **replace traditional RNNs with transformer-based architectures** for scalability and performance.

---

# 4. Generative AI and Large Language Models

Concepts:

- Generative models (GANs, VAEs)  
- Large Language Models (LLMs) and fine-tuning  
- Prompt engineering and system integration  
- Text, code, and content generation  

Applications:

- AI content generation  
- automated code completion  
- creative problem-solving tools  

---

# 5. Multi-Modal AI Systems

- Combine text, image, audio, and structured data  
- Build AI systems that **process multiple modalities** simultaneously  
- Applications: visual question answering, autonomous systems, AI assistants  

Architecture example:

```

Text Encoder → Fusion Layer ← Image Encoder
↓
Multi-modal Output

```

# 6. Reinforcement Learning at Scale

- Deep Reinforcement Learning (Deep Q-Learning, Policy Gradients)  
- Environment simulation and training at scale  
- Applications: robotics, autonomous vehicles, game-playing AI  

---

# 7. Model Optimization and Compression

- Quantization and pruning for model efficiency  
- Knowledge distillation for smaller deployable models  
- Hardware-aware model optimization  
- Ensures **low-latency inference and high throughput**  

---

# 8. Scalable AI System Design

- Distributed training across multi-GPU/TPU clusters  
- Parallel inference and micro-batching  
- Cloud and edge deployment strategies  
- Designing AI pipelines for **millions of users**  

---

# 9. Deployment of Advanced AI Models

- API and microservice deployment for advanced AI  
- Real-time and batch inference systems  
- Monitoring latency, throughput, and reliability  
- Integrating multi-modal and generative AI into products  

---

# 10. AI System Monitoring and Observability

- Logging, metrics, and tracing for deep learning systems  
- Drift detection and model retraining triggers  
- Alerts for performance degradation and failure  

---

# 11. AI Performance Engineering

- Profiling GPU/TPU usage  
- Optimizing memory and computation  
- Parallelization and asynchronous execution  

---

# 12. Security and Robustness in AI Systems

- Secure handling of sensitive data  
- Ensuring reliability of AI-driven critical systems
