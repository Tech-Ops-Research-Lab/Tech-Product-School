# Phase 9 — AI Product Engineering

## AI Engineering Product School Curriculum

Phase 9 transitions from infrastructure operation to **end-to-end engineering of AI-powered products**. Focus centers on designing, building, validating, and scaling technology products where AI models operate as core system components.

AI product engineering integrates software engineering, machine learning systems, user experience design, and product management into a single engineering discipline.

Engineers construct **complete AI-powered technology platforms**, capable of delivering measurable value to users and organizations.

# 2. Foundations of AI Product Engineering

AI product engineering integrates multiple disciplines:

machine learning engineering
backend software engineering
data engineering
product design
system reliability engineering

An AI product differs from traditional software because system behavior is partially driven by statistical models rather than deterministic code.

Key characteristics:

dependency on training pipelines
dynamic performance behavior
